About Details

Where Creativity Meets Precision

Details is not just a design studio—it's a philosophy. Founded by Andrew Nabil, a creative designer with over 11 years of professional experience, Details specializes in transforming brands through meticulous visual storytelling and innovative design solutions.

At Details, we believe that exceptional design lies in the nuances—the careful selection of colors, the perfect balance of typography, and the strategic placement of every element. Our approach combines artistic vision with strategic thinking to create designs that don't just look beautiful, but also drive results and leave lasting impressions.

With expertise spanning brand identity, social media design, motion graphics, and visual communications, we've helped countless clients across various industries establish their unique visual presence. Every project is an opportunity to push creative boundaries while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and quality.

Based in Egypt and serving clients globally, Details delivers comprehensive design solutions that resonate with audiences and elevate brands to new heights. We don't just create designs—we craft experiences that matter.
